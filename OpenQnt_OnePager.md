# OpenQnt (Open Quant)

**Vision:** The future of investing is agentic. OpenQnt provides an autonomous AI asset manager and unified quant platform that analyzes macro reports, political shifts, and whale activity 24/7. 

## Problem
Manual trading is a legacy constraint. The human brain cannot simultaneously process thousands of news articles, policy changes, whale movements, and technical signals—let alone retain them under pressure. Furthermore, traditional systems bolt AI on as an afterthought. As a result, investors and institutions suffer from emotional interference, slow execution speeds, and fragmented tooling that separates research from real-time execution.

## Potential
OpenQnt acts as **"Legora" but for finance institutions**, bridging the gap between bespoke enterprise infrastructure and broad accessibility. It enables users to visually snap together components to automate complex financial processes.
- **Visual Strategy Builder:** An intuitive, drag-and-drop workflow that abstracts away complex programming. Users can fuse quant algorithms with AI agents and backtest strategies without writing code.
- **Next-Generation Terminal:** The Bloomberg terminal has dominated the industry for decades, yet it remains largely manual and lacks true intelligent automation. OpenQnt aims to fix this by embedding autonomous agents that can execute tasks, debate each other to stress-test theses, and make sub-second decisions on global news.
- **Cost Revolution:** While traditional funds and asset managers take a heavy percentage of AUM, OpenQnt users only pay a fixed/fractional cost based on the LLM tokens used by the agents.
- **Target Market:** Asset management companies, Private Equity (PE) firms, Venture Capital (VC) companies, other financial institutions, and even retail traders.
- **Customization Strategy:** Our roadmap focuses on tailoring the application for each institutional customer's specific operational workflows.

## Traction & Vision
- **Functional MVP Developed:** Successfully built a comprehensive platform capable of backtesting, live execution, and AI-driven automation.
- **Autonomous Optimization Engine:** Engineered an operational self-improving loop where the platform autonomously refines strategies, actively demonstrating the core value proposition of AI in quantitative finance.
- **Market Ready Infrastructure:** Established execution pathways with major retail and institutional brokers (Alpaca, Interactive Brokers, IG), proving the viability of the infrastructure.
- **The Roadmap Ahead:** We are transitioning from an advanced, powerful prototype toward a scalable B2B solution. The immediate goal is to onboard pilot institutional clients to test and refine our custom workflows.
